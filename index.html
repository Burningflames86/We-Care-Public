Chem Nomenclature (For Medical Students)

Our product is a tool that helps students name hydrocarbons. Hydrocarbons are compounds made up of hydrogen and carbon atoms, and learning the names and structures of different hydrocarbons can be a challenge for students. Our product simplifies this process by providing a platform that allows students to input the molecular structure of a hydrocarbon and receive the correct name in return. By providing an easy-to-use tool for naming hydrocarbons, our product helps students learn and understand this important subject more effectively.
